About Me

I build Flutter apps that are designed to last, not just to launch.

From architecture and state management to polished UI and the smallest interaction details, I focus on building products that are fast, scalable, and easy to maintain. Every screen, component, and architectural decision is made with long term growth in mind, so adding new features or fixing bugs does not mean rewriting half the application.

Clean Architecture is more than a pattern I follow. It is how I keep projects organized, reusable, and easy to extend as requirements evolve. I enjoy turning complex ideas into intuitive user experiences while writing code that is clear, testable, and built for the next developer who works on the project.

Whether I am building an MVP, improving an existing application, or helping untangle a codebase that has become difficult to maintain, my goal is always the same. Deliver reliable software that keeps moving forward instead of slowing down as it grows.

Outside of development, you will usually find me with a cup of coffee, learning something new, or refining the small details that turn a good app into a great one.
